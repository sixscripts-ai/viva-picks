PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Viva Picks ("we", "us", "our") operates vivapicks.tech. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you access our sports analysis and signal platform.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide our intelligence services:

  • Account Data: Email address and encrypted account credentials.
  • Payment Metadata: Securely processed by third-party providers (Stripe). We do not store full credit card data.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device data, usage analytics and cookies.

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Your data is used strictly for operational needs:

  • Account creation, management, and authentication.
  • Providing picks, analysis, and real-time signal broadcasts.
  • Processing subscription billing and access controls.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

4. COOKIES

We use essential cookies required for site functionality (maintaining your session) and optional analytics cookies to improve our models. Non-essential cookies require your consent.

5. DATA SHARING & SECURITY

We do not sell personal data. Information is only shared with trusted partners necessary for service delivery:

  • Stripe: For PCI-compliant payment processing.
  • Nodemailer/External SMTP: For signal delivery and account alerts.

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. USER RIGHTS

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time. You may terminate your account and wipe your data via the profile management dashboard.
